# About Luminous Music Player

🍁 Created by [Eric Soltys](https://esoltys.github.io/), a Canadian software developer and a hobbyist creating art and music in the BC Kootenays.

## Tech Stack

- Backend: [Rust](https://www.rust-lang.org/) and [Tauri](https://tauri.app/)
- Frontend: [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/) and [Svelte](https://svelte.dev/Svelte)
- Database: [SQLite](https://sqlite.org/)
- https://github.com/pdeljanov/Symphonia for audio decoding
- https://github.com/HEnquist/rubato for audio resampling
- https://github.com/RustAudio/cpal for audio output
